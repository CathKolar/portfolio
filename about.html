About me:

I am a Front-End Developer currently based in Seville, Spain.

Born and raised in northern Minnesota, I moved to Spain in 2015.

While I am currently working with React and Next.js, I am always seeking to broaden my horizons. An ongoing passion project of mine is reading up on and implementing more accessible features in my work with the hopes of making the digital world as equitable as possible. With ample experience in editing as an EFL teacher I am not afraid of a little refactoring!

Having lived in the US, Finland and Spain, and worked in international education both as a teacher and administrator, I possess the soft skills needed exel in intercultural communication. An absolute must in today's globalized world.

When I am not coding you can probably find me dreaming up recipes, on the hunt for a coffee, or listening NPR or a podcast. Sometimes all of the above!

Let's work together! Drop me a line for work inquiries or to just say hi!
